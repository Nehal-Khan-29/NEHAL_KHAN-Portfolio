AEyeD - Artificial Eye Decipher

AEyeD is an affordable, AI-powered real-time monitoring system designed to assist visually impaired individuals. Combining Computer Vision and IoT, it enables safe navigation through face and gesture recognition, vehicle and stair detection, and text-to-speech feedback. Built using CNNs, Haar Cascade, OpenCV, Keras, and pytesseract, AEyeD runs on a Raspberry Pi with voice-command face labeling and OCR-based audio output. Tested on patients with Retinitis Pigmentosa, it achieved an F1 score of 0.88, significantly improving situational awareness and autonomy.
